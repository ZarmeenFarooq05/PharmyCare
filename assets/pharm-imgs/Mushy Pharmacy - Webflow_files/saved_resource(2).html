Meet Your Pharmacist

Malek Musheinesh, PharmD, received his degree from Wayne State University and has been dedicated to healthcare in Southeast Michigan for nearly a decade. Specializing in Medication Therapy Management (MTM), Immunization, and comprehensive patient care, he has made a significant impact.

Serving as a board member at the largest underserved clinic in the state, HUDA Clinic, Malek centers his profession on ensuring optimal medications for his patients' medical conditions. With a passion for patient care, he brings expertise and compassion to every aspect of his pharmacy practice.
